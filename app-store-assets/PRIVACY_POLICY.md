
# Privacy Policy for Aura

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Aura ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Aura stores the following data locally on your device only:
- Your selected language preference
- Notification time settings
- Day streak counter
- Last app open date

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:
- iOS: Keychain and UserDefaults
- Android: SharedPreferences

## Third-Party Services

Aura does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Notifications

If you enable notifications, Aura will send local notifications to your device at your chosen time. These notifications are generated locally and do not involve any external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Aura does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our website.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: support@auraapp.com
- Website: https://your-website.com

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers. You can delete all local app data by uninstalling the app.

## Data Security

All data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security features. We recommend keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric authentication.

## International Users

Aura is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data crosses international borders.

## Consent

By using Aura, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
